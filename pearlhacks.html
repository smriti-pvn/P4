Safe Escape

HTML | CSS | Javascript | Bootstrap

Learn, share and love where you are!

Safe Escape allows you to immerse yourself in cultures that we cannot explore during this time! Listen to music specifically curated for you to enjoy the full cultural experience and scan the EchoAR codes we've added to bring the world's most famous tourist attractions to your home!

This hack won Amazon's Most Customer Obsessed Hack at UNC Pearl Hacks 2021 for thinking of the customer first and then working backwards while keeping the end-users in mind.
